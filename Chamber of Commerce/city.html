The City of Spanish Fork

Nestled at the mouth of Spanish Fork canyon. Spanish Fork is a beautiful city that lies in southern Utah County.

Being the 21st largest city in all of Utah. Spanish Fork has a population of 42,895 according to the current census.

The average household income in Spanish Fork is $91,446 with a poverty rate of 6.91%. The median rental costs in recent years comes to $1,128 per month, and the median house value is $273,600.

    Spanish Fork Demographics According to the most recent ACS, the racial composition of Spanish Fork was:

  • White: 94.21%
  • Two or more races: 2.36%
  • Other race: 1.53%
  • Native Hawaiian or Pacific Islander: 0.85%
  • Native American: 0.57%
  • Black or African American: 0.28%
  • Asian: 0.20%
