Target Audience

The Target Audience for this site is anyone that lives in a climate that has tornados. It is for first responders, business', and the public.

Personas

Ross has a family and living in the suburbs of Joplin Missouri. He has built a home and raised a wonderful family. He has always been terrified of tornados and has a top of the line bunker.

Julie thinks tornados are amazing. She loves to learn about them. She has always wondered what makes a tornado.

Scenarios

1. A family of 6 is nervous about the upcoming tornado season. They would like to know ahead of time if there are any tornados in their area.

2. A vistor to the midwest would like to look and see what the forcast of the area is like. He would also like to see if any tornados are possible while he is there.

3. An avid learner of everything would love to know how a tornado forms and why it is so devastating. They would also like to know why the sky turns green.

4. A homeowner needs to put in a shelter and do some wind damage repairs for his home. He can look and see what the best recommended shelters are and who could help with the project.

5. Missionaries servering in a tornado area would like to see if anyone has any damage to their home. They would like to find service opportunities.
