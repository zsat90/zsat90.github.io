Contrast

Workmax

Workmax

You can see from this website that they use many different contrasting colors. I think this helps seperate everything nicely. They also use contrasting typography. This helps with focusing on the words that are important.

Visual Hierarchy

Amazon

Amazon

Visual Hierarchy is important in web pages. It puts the most important information first. Amazon is probably one of the best at this. You can scroll down from most important information to least important. From the image everyone knows exactly how far to scroll down in order to see the similar items.

Repetition

Door Dash

Door Dash

This is a great example of repetition. You can see this when looking at the colors on the pages as well as the colors of the buttons. You can also notice this when looking at the pictures they have of the food. They appear high quality.
